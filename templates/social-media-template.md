# AI Content Factory — Social Media Content Template

> 用途：生成跨平台一致性、高参与度社媒内容  
> 适用平台：Twitter/X、LinkedIn、Instagram Reels、TikTok、YouTube Shorts  
> 核心原则：每条内容服务一个目的（触达/互动/转化）

---

| 内容类型 | 目的 | 发布频率 | 平台 |

| Value Thread（价值线程） | 建立权威 | 1–2×/周 | Twitter, LinkedIn |

| User Proof（用户证言） | 社会认同 | 1×/周 | Twitter, LinkedIn |

## 🎯 AI 填充指令（Prompt）

### 生成 Hook Post 系列

```markdown
请为以下主题生成 5 个不同风格的 Hook Post：

主题：[YOUR TOPIC]
目标平台：[TWITTER / LINKEDIN / INSTAGRAM]
目标受众：[AUDIENCE]
每条帖子目的：[AWARENESS / ENGAGEMENT / TRAFFIC]

要求：
- Hook 必须在首行/前3秒抓住注意力
- 每条风格不同（争议/数据/故事/提问/反常识）
- Twitter：≤ 280 字符，含 1-2 相关 hashtag
- LinkedIn：150–300 字符，结尾引发讨论
- 每条包含 1 个自然插入的 CTA（reply / share / click link）
```

### 生成 Value Thread

```markdown
请生成一个 [N] 条的 Twitter/LinkedIn Thread：

Thread 主题：[THREAD TOPIC]
核心信息：[CORE INSIGHT]
目标受众：[AUDIENCE]
目标行动：[DESIRED ACTION - follow / visit / subscribe]

格式要求：
- Thread 第 1 条：超级 Hook（引发好奇）
- Thread 第 2-N 条：层层递进，每个 reply 一个独立观点
- Thread 最后 1 条：总结 + CTA
- 全程保持[CONVERSATIONAL / AUTHORITATIVE / CASUAL]语气
- 使用简洁有力的句子，每条不超过 280 字符
- 添加 1-2 个相关 hashtag
```

### 生成短视频脚本（30–60秒）

```markdown
请为 [PLATFORM] Shorts 生成脚本：

视频主题：[TOPIC]
时长：[30 SECONDS / 60 SECONDS]
视频目的：[AWARENESS / EDUCATION / ENTERTAINMENT]
CTA 类型：[FOLLOW / LINK_CLICK / COMMENT]

脚本结构：
- Hook（前 3 秒）：必须抓住注意力
- Body（中间）：核心内容，节奏快
- CTA（最后 5 秒）：明确行动指引

格式：
[0:00-0:03] HOOK: [TEXT]
[0:03-0:XX] BODY: [TEXT]
[0:XX-0:XX] CTA: [TEXT]

语气：[CASUAL / ENERGETIC / EDUCATED / FUNNY]
```

### LinkedIn Article Hook（长帖）

```
主题：Why Most AI Content Fails

---

The problem with most "AI-generated content" isn't the AI.

It's the human who doesn't know how to use it.

Here's what I see every week:

❌ Prompt → Copy → Paste → Publish
❌ "Write me a blog post about X"
❌ Using the same generic prompt for everything

These approaches produce mediocre content at best.

Here's what ACTUALLY works:

🔑 1. Treat AI as a collaborator, not a replacement

The best writers I know use AI for:
- Research synthesis (saving 3-4 hours)
- First-draft structure (saving 2 hours)
- Headline variation (saving 30 min)

Then they add what AI can't: personal stories, original insights, their actual voice.

🔑 2. The 70/30 rule

I aim for:
- 70% original thinking (insights, analysis, stories)
- 30% AI assistance (research, structure, variation)

This ratio produces content that sounds like YOU, not everyone else.

🔑 3. Edit like your reputation depends on it (because it does)

Every AI output needs human refinement:
- Remove fluff
- Add specifics (numbers, names, examples)
- Inject personality

Your audience can tell. Google can tell.

The writers who thrive with AI aren't the ones using it most.
They're the ones who know exactly when to use it and when to think for themselves.

What's your AI editing process? 👇

#ContentStrategy #AI #WritingCommunity

---
```
